## Condition number


$$
CN = \frac{\frac{f(x) - f(x_0)}{f(x_0)}}{\frac{x-x_0}{x_0}} \approx \frac{x_0 f'(x_0)}{f(x_0)}
$$

Matrix case:

$$
cond(A) = \frac{||A||}{||A^{-1}||}
$$
